About Me

Hello, welcome to my page! My name is Cindy.

I was born and raised in the Bay Area, specifically Oakland—been here pretty much all my life.

I did relocate to South Florida for 18 months for my first job out of college. But since I missed my friends and family terribly, I was allowed to move back home. I still work for that very same company.

I like to travel and experience what it’s like to live in other parts of the world. I still have many countries in which I would like to visit, but so far I have been to: Canada, China, Japan, France, and Singapore. I’ve traveled to many cities within the United States as well, but only ones along the Eastern and Western coasts. One of my bucket list items is to do a road trip and travel through all 48 contiguous states.
